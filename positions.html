Exciting Opportunities

I am excited to announce that I will be starting a new position soon, and I am actively seeking motivated researchers to join my team. We are looking for passionate individuals at various levels to contribute to our cutting-edge research projects. Generally, send your materials to Dr. Linna An (linnaan@rice.com), and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

We welcome applicants from all nationalities, race, etc who are interested in protein design and biotechnology, no previous experience needed. Working visa will be supported. Please contact Rice ISSS if you have living, visa, or other immigration questions.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Available Positions

Graduate Researchers

Position Requirements

  • No previous computational experience needed, but you need to be willing to learn computation
  • Preference of academic or industrial experience in chemistry, biochemistry, computational or related fields
  • Willing to work independently and in teams

How to apply? Send below materials to Dr. Linna An (linnaan@rice.edu)

  • Feel free to contact before or after your graduate school application. If already applied to Rice Graduate College, do make a note so Dr. An can pull your file out. For rotation students, please contact directly.
  • CV
  • Undergrad transcripts (unofficial is good enough)
  • A paragraph of your past research summary, if any.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome applications from postdoctoral researchers who want to advance their research careers.

How to apply? Send below materials to Dr. Linna An (linnaan@rice.edu)

  • CV
  • Cover letter (at least one paragraph summarize your past work, at least one paragraph describe your future plan. You are more than welcome to talk about your future interests/proposals in future plans. At most 2 pages.)
  • Name and contact info of three references

Undergraduate Researchers

We are looking for undergraduate students who are interested in gaining hands-on research experience.

Position Requirements

  • Passion
  • Willingness to learn and collaborate

How to apply? Send below materials to Dr. Linna An (linnaan@rice.edu)

  • CV
  • Up-to-date unofficial transcripts
  • (Optional) A paragraph of your past research summary if any
