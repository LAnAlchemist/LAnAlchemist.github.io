Research: Functional Protein Design

Nature takes millions of years to evolve functional proteins. Then traditional protein engineers need to spend years to engineer native proteins to desired functions. Sometimes, they work. Mostly, they don't.

Using protein design, the 2024 Nobel prize-winning technology, we can fast-forward the evolution process and push the biomedical research and industry forward.
